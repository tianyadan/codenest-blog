# Work Diary 2

## Notes

Today I adapted the mobile UI for the scheduling module and basically forced a perfectly normal admin system into an H5 page. I really have to say the idea was unexpectedly creative. Tomorrow we are releasing a microservices project, and it is going straight to production without testing, so I honestly do not feel confident about it. I want to follow a proper software development process, but most people skip it and only care about going live quickly. Only now do I fully understand how important real testing is.
Every role has its value.
