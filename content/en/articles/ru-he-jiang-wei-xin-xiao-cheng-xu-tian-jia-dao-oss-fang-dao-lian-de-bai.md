# How to Add a WeChat Mini Program to the OSS Hotlink Protection Allowlist

## Notes

The fixed domain for WeChat Mini Programs is:
`https://servicewechat.com`

You need to add it to the allowlist before a WeChat Mini Program can access OSS images. The same idea also applies to COS (Tencent Cloud).
